About this Dashboard ▼

This dashboard summarises survey responses from the I-REACCH evaluation study. Use it to track patterns in research culture sentiment and generate evidence for team discussions.

Step 1 — Select a dataset. Use the drop-down in the controls panel to view responses for a specific survey wave.

Step 2 — Read the numbers. The KPIs and distribution chart show overall sentiment. The summary panel translates this into plain-language findings with suggested actions.

Step 3 — Export or share. Use Export to Excel to create a file ready for further analysis, or copy the summary for a report or team meeting.

        Methods & definitions
        • Scores: 1..5 Likert mapped from text labels (e.g., 1=“Extremely unvalued”, 5=“Extremely valued”).
        • % Positive: share of scored responses that are 4 or 5. % Negative: share that are 1 or 2. Base size shown as n.
        • Small n: if n<30, a “small n” tag appears — interpret cautiously.
        • Higher / Lower in Q2: a difference of ≥5 percentage points that also passes a two-proportion test (p<.05); otherwise shown as “Small difference”.
        • Theme extraction: simple tokenization with stopwords removed; top frequent terms; theme drift = overlap vs new vs dropped terms. Quotes are anonymised.
        • Average/median: reported descriptively; medians are preferred for skewed distributions.
