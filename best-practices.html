How to browse and contribute ▼

Step 1 — Browse. Use the search and filter tools below to find practices from other Schools and Colleges. Click any tag on a card to filter by that theme.

Step 2 — Vote. On each card, tell us whether your School does something similar, is considering it, has had mixed results, or finds it not applicable. This helps others understand how widely a practice is used across the University.

Step 3 — Share. Use the button below to add a practice from your own School. Your submission is reviewed by the WS5 team before it appears publicly.

Mentoring circles for early-career researchers

Example
Doctoral College / PGR Established

Monthly peer circles of 4–6 early-career researchers with a rotating facilitator drawn from the group. Sessions are 60 minutes, structured around a shared agenda (one challenge, one win, one resource to share). No senior staff attend, keeping the space low-pressure. Requires minimal coordination — a shared calendar invite and a short briefing document.
